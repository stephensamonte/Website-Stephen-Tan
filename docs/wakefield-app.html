Wakefield High School News

The purpose of Wakefield High School News is to keep community members informed of event happening at Wakefield High School.

This is what the product does.

My Vision

I hope that the Wakefield HS News mobile applications will be a place the community can go to to keep up with Wakefield Events and be a launching platform for student creations. The application collects and categorizes tweets from Wakefield making it easier to find news. Additionally, no Twitter sign in so anyone with an Android or iPhone can keep up with events.

In the "My News" section, users can follow twitter feeds (such as their class twitter feed or certain clubs) of their choosing.
The applications can be downloaded on the android and iOS market by looking up "Wakefield High School News"

How it Works

The application collects Twitter feeds from the Twitter account @cwakefield_app. So who ever has access to this account can add and remove people/groups from appearing in the application's "home" feed.

Additional features can be added to this applicaiton thorugh updates.
